Support

Hi. How can we help?

Timebase is a small app made by one person. There's no helpdesk, no ticketing system, and no automated reply. Just email hi@amrith.co with the details, and you'll hear back within a couple of days.

Before you do, the answers below cover almost every question that's come up so far. Have a quick look — your question might already be here.

How do I scrub through time?

Drag vertically anywhere on the main world clock screen. Up moves time forward, down moves it backward. The pill at the bottom shows how far you've scrubbed; tap any city — or the pill itself — to snap back to "now."

How do I add a city?

Tap the + in Settings → Cities, then search for the city by name. Timebase ships with thousands of cities. You can have up to 8 active in the world clock list at once.

How do I change my home city?

Settings → Cities → swipe (or long-press) any city → "Make home." The home city appears with a 🏠 marker and anchors the Live Activity countdown.

Why isn't Up Next showing my events?

Up Next needs Calendar access. If you skipped or denied the permission, open Settings → Privacy & Security → Calendars → Timebase and turn it on. The app will pick up your events the next time it launches.

Why isn't weather showing for a city?

Timebase uses Apple's WeatherKit. If a city has never been queried before, the first request can take a few seconds; if WeatherKit is temporarily unavailable, the detail sheet falls back to time + daylight info without weather. The weather pill returns automatically once Apple's service responds.

How do I add a widget?

Long-press the home screen → tap + → search "Timebase." You'll see two families: World Clock (small / medium / large + lock-screen accessories) and Up Next (countdowns). Tap any widget to drop it; tap-and-hold to edit if you ever want to swap variants.

Where does the Live Activity come from?

When one of your calendar events is within 1 hour, Timebase starts a Live Activity automatically — it appears on the lock screen as a countdown card and on the Dynamic Island as a small pill. Tap to open Up Next. It ends automatically when the event starts.

Does Timebase sync between my iPhone and iPad?

Yes, automatically, as long as both devices are signed into the same iCloud account and you haven't disabled Timebase in iCloud Settings.

How do I reset everything?

Settings → Danger → Reset all data. This clears your cities, preferences, and onboarding state on this device and in iCloud. Calendar access is managed by iOS — the same Settings page also has a Disconnect Calendar shortcut that deeplinks to iOS Settings for revoking the system-level permission.

Still stuck?

Email hi@amrith.co with:

  • Your iOS version (Settings → General → About)
  • Timebase version (app's Settings screen, bottom footer)
  • What you tried, and what happened instead
